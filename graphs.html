The groups who posted about the Ukrainian war since 2022Q2

The two graphs illustrate the distribution of Facebook posts about Ukraine, emphasizing the roles of politics, media, and society. When examining political engagement, the graph on the left represents global activity, while the graph on the right focuses specifically on Denmark.
