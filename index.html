What You Can Do to Show Support for Ukraine

1. Donate to Verified Organizations

Support humanitarian aid by donating to trusted organizations like UNICEF, The Danish Ukraine committee, or the Red Cross. Your contribution can make a real difference.

2. Display Ukraine’s Colors

Show solidarity on social media or in your community by displaying Ukraine’s national colors—blue and yellow. This small gesture helps raise awareness.

3. Spread Awareness

Share information, stories, and news about the ongoing situation in Ukraine. Educating others helps maintain global attention and keeps the support alive.

4. Support Ukrainian Businesses

Purchase goods or services from Ukrainian businesses online. This supports the country’s economy and shows solidarity with its people.

5. Join Peaceful Protests

Participate in peaceful demonstrations and events to show collective support for Ukraine and raise your voice for peace and freedom.

6. Reach Out to Local Politicians

Encourage your local leaders to continue supporting Ukraine through humanitarian aid, resources, and diplomatic efforts.
